Why alpaca clothing!

The benefits of alpaca clothing:

  • The clothes are super soft, sometimes I even sleep in them because they are just so comfy I never want to take them off!
  • I can workout in them and go hiking and it wicks away the sweat. You don't even need to wash them because they are odor resitant.
  • Feeling a bit bloated? No worries they are also stretchy!
  • I have had my items from a long time and they still look really good because of their durable knit.
  • We all know the environment is something we need to look after but sometimes its hard to find clothing that is both sustainable and looks good! Well this is both as it is biodegradable.
  • Also it will stop you overheating because it is a natural temperature regulator
